Monkey And Bear.

At lunchtime Monkey and Bear stopped being friends.

All because of two gingernut cookies.

‘Those cookies are mine,’ said Monkey

‘No! They’re not,’ said Bear

‘Whatever,’ shouted Monkey and he grabbed the plate.

Monkey threw his comb, sunglasses, scarf and cookies into his saucepan.

‘Hey!’ cried Bear ‘Where are you going?

I’m going to travel the world and have ADVENTURES.’ Said Monkey

‘But you can’t go without me,’ cried Bear.

‘Yes, I can,’ said Monkey.

Monkey dragged his saucepan down to the beach and off he sailed.

Monkey never looked back, so he never saw Bear paddling behind in the saucepan lid.

All day long Monkey watched dolphins play and seagulls fly but when a thick fog rolled in Monkey was scared. Waves crashed against his saucepan. A howling, growling wind blew him backwards. His scarf got soggy. His tail got wet. A seagull flew off with his sunglasses and he couldn’t find his comb. ‘I want to go home,’ he cried.

Then through the fog Monkey saw a tiny island and sitting under a leafy Kumquat tree was BEAR!

‘MONKEY!’ Yelled Bear

‘BEAR!’ Shouted Monkey and he paddled as fast as he could towards the island when….

Something dreadful happened!

Something MOVED beneath his saucepan. Something SLUDGY, SLIMY and HUGE.
