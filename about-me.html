About Me

I embarked on my artistic journey at Camberwell School of Art in London, where I obtained my degree in Graphic Design with a specialisation in illustration.

Beyond my artistic pursuits, I'm passionate about playing bridge. My husband and I run The Lavender Bridge Club in Wandsworth, London, UK, where I call home. I discovered bridge during a challenging period of illness, and it proved to be a remarkable aid in my recovery. Like the stroke of a brush on canvas, bridge allowed me to regain my sense of purpose and connection.

I'm honoured to be a member of the Society of Authors and Illustrators, where I continue to learn, grow, and connect with fellow creatives who share my passion for storytelling and art. I invite you to join me on this exciting creative journey as I explore the world of fiction and dive deeper into writing and illustrating picture books. Together, let's celebrate the magic of stories, the beauty of art, and the resilience of the human spirit.
